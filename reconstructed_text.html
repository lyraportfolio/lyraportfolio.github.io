Inspired by a scene from Italo Calvino's 'If on a winter's night a traveler', this is a script that can remove articles and prepositions from the text in a pdf or epub file, then provide a list of the words and amount at which they occur, and then scale that list back up into the format of the original text.

Examples of this can be found here:
Howl
The Electric Kool-Aid Acid test
The Old Man and The Sea
